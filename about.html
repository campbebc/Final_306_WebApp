Our Philosophy

We create products and services that have the ability to improve upon things that we care about as a collective team. These products and services are designed to be very clean, simple, and easy for the clients to use.

Our Mission

Party Playlist is a mobile and web application that increases communication between party-goers and the DJ. Our intuitive voting system allows for users to select the songs THEY want to hear while our groundbreaking accelerometer hardware provides the DJ with invaluable feedback on how the crowd reacted to their talents.

Our Team

Our team consists of 14 students enrolled in ISAT 306: Data Communications and Telecom Lab at James Madison University in Harrisonburg, Va. We were organized into front-end, central, back-end, and integration teams in order to complete the task at hand of creating an end-to-end service that incorporates physical computing hardware.
